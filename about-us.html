About Us

James Douglas is more than a search firm. We are a leadership advisory movement-built on the legacy of EMA Partners in India, and renewed with a sharper purpose: to create value through people.
We believe that people shape organizations, and organizations shape society. Our role is to stand at the intersection of ambition and impact, partnering with leaders and organizations at moments that define the future.

Our Mission
To empower organizations and leaders through trusted advisory, meaningful partnerships, and people who make a difference.
Our Vision

To redefine leadership advisory by shaping teams and careers that move industries and communities forward.
