Cookie Policy

Last Updated: September 15, 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how James Douglas uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes, including:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website.
  • Functional Cookies: These enable enhanced functionality and personalization.
  • Targeting Cookies: These may be set through our site by our advertising partners.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

The table below explains the cookies we use and why we use them:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
session_id Maintains your session on our website Session Essential
_ga Google Analytics - Used to distinguish users 2 years Performance
_gid Google Analytics - Used to distinguish users 24 hours Performance
_gat_gtag Google Analytics - Used to throttle request rate 1 minute Performance
cookie_consent Stores your cookie preferences 1 year Functional

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. These may include:

  • Google Analytics
  • LinkedIn Analytics
  • Social media plugins

We do not control these third-party cookies. Please refer to the respective privacy policies of these third parties for more information.

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies you've got and delete them on an individual basis
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from particular sites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

However, please note that if you choose to disable cookies, certain features of our website may not function properly.

6. Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner where you can manage your cookie preferences. You can change these preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our website.

7. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our cookie practices.
